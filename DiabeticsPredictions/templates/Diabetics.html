Diabetics Prediction

Diabetes is a common chronic disease and poses a great threat to human health. The characteristic of diabetes is that the blood glucose is higher than the normal level, which is caused by defective insulin secretion or its impaired biological effects, or both. We will be predicting that whether the patient has diabetes or not on the basis of the features we will provide to our machine learning model.
